## Definition
Security models and threat frameworks help organizations understand how to protect systems, data, and networks from cyber threats.

## Assets
Assets are anything valuable that needs protection.
- Tangible assets include servers and laptops.
- Intangible assets include customer data and company reputation.

## Vulnerabilities
Vulnerabilities are weaknesses in systems or processes that attackers can exploit.

## Threats
Threats are events or actions that can harm assets.

## Risk
Risk is the probability of a threat exploiting a vulnerability and causing impact.

## CIA Triad
The CIA Triad is a foundational cybersecurity model.

### Confidentiality
Protects data from unauthorized access.

### Integrity
Ensures data is not altered improperly.

### Availability
Ensures systems and data remain accessible.
