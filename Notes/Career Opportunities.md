# Career Opportunities in IT Security

## Outline
- What is Cybersecurity
- Red Team vs Blue Team
- Cybersecurity Career Opportunities

## Definition
Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computer systems, networks, and sensitive data from theft, damage, and unauthorized access.

## Red Team vs Blue Team
<img width="300" height="168" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/94903342-5820-461d-86ec-62d26fdfc67e" />

### Red Team
- Offensive security specialists
- Simulate cyberattacks
- Identify vulnerabilities before attackers do

### Blue Team
- Defensive security specialists
- Monitor systems and network traffic
- Protect organizations from threats

## Key Points
- Cybersecurity jobs are in high demand.
- Roles include SOC Analyst, Penetration Tester, Threat Hunter, and DFIR Analyst.
- The three pillars of cybersecurity are People, Process, and Technology.
